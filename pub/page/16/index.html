  • ROCCER: A ROC convex hull rule learning algorithm

    Prati, R and Flach, P

    Publication date: February 21, 2011

    Journal/Conference: Proc. ECML/PKDD Workshop Advances in Inductive Rule Learning

    In this paper we propose a method to construct rule sets that have a convex hull in ROC space. We introduce a rule selection algorithm called ROCCER, which operates by selecting rules from a larger set of rules in or- der to optimise Area Under the ROC Curve (AUC). Compared with set covering algorithms, our method is less dependent on the previously induced rules. Exper- imental results on three UCI datasets show significant improvements on two of these.

    More info: http://www.ke.tu-darmstadt.de/events/ECML-PKDD-04-WS/Proceedings/prati.pdf
